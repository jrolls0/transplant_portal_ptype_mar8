# UI Audit

Review the UI like a design critic and UX reviewer.

## Audit checklist
1. Does the screen have a clear visual hierarchy?
2. Does anything look generic, templated, or default-styled?
3. Is spacing deliberate and consistent?
4. Is the density right for the job?
5. Are there too many borders, cards, badges, or decorative elements?
6. Is the typography doing enough work?
7. Are there responsiveness issues?
8. Are contrast, focus states, and readability acceptable?
9. What are the top 5 highest-leverage improvements?

## Output
- Lead with the most important issues.
- Be specific.
- Prefer surgical fixes over a total rewrite unless necessary.
